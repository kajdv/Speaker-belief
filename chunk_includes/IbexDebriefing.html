Experiment Debriefing:


With this experiment, we're investigating various issues in how elements like negation influences people's understanding and judgment of sentences involving predicates like `believe' and `say'. In the experiment, you saw dialogues involving sentences like the following:

    ...however, John said that Lisa and Mike broke up.

We're conducting a large scale investigation looking at the kinds of inferences that are licensed by sentences like this one, and how different combinations of elements in the sentences influence their acceptability.

The results will help us understand how speakers take into account the meaning of different words when they evaluate the context in which a sentence occurs.

On a more general level, this study will inform current debates on how different aspects of meanings influences the types of grammatical contexts that a verb can occur in. It will also help us to design further experiments to understand how words and sentences are interpreted in online language processing.
